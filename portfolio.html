Introduction

This case study is an exploration of the process I undertook to create the very portfolio you are currently reading. It delves into the steps, challenges, and creative decisions that shaped its development, offering valuable insights into my journey of crafting an engaging and informative showcase of my work.

Research Question

How may I share my professional skills and highlight the uniqueness of my work on a portfolio?

The how in the question above suggests that every idea I come up with is merely a possibility and shouldn't be taken as a final decision but rather an indication of what might work. Following the .dot framework, I focused on the field research by exploring what was already done, not only by other professionals, but primarily by previous students, who have also built a portfolio as part of their semester assignment.

From using a CMS...

After much contemplation, I made the decision to utilize Squarespace as the platform for building my portfolio, stepping away from the initial notion of coding it myself. This shift allowed me to streamline the development process and focus on presenting my work effectively. The resulting portfolio exudes a minimalist and simple aesthetic, aligning with my design principles. Choosing Squarespace was primarily driven by time considerations, recognizing that coding it from scratch within the given time limitations would be challenging. By leveraging the user-friendly features and templates offered by Squarespace, I was able to create a visually appealing and professional portfolio that effectively showcases my skills and projects.

To coding it myself!

Despite being initially frightned and worried about developing the portfolio myself,I made a pivotal decision to transition from Squarespace and code my portfolio myself. This shift was not an easy one, as I initially had concerns about the time and complexity involved. However, I recognized that by coding it myself, I would have complete control over the design and functionality, allowing me to truly leverage my coding abilities.

For the construction of this portfolio, I relied on the fundamental building blocks of web development: HTML, CSS, and Vanilla JavaScript. Having a basic understanding of these technologies, I found them to be perfectly suited for the task at hand. Given the simplicity of the portfolio's design, I was able to achieve impressive results by leveraging the core functionalities of these languages. This approach allowed me to maintain full control over the code and tailor it precisely to my needs. Harnessing the power of HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, I crafted a polished and functional portfolio that effectively showcases my work and capabilities.
Feel free to check the source code on git here.

Explaining the iteration

During the transition to coding my portfolio myself, I took the opportunity for a complete rebranding, shifting from the previous dark-themed design to a lighter and more refreshing version. However, I made sure to maintain the minimal and elegant style that truly reflects my personality. The design choices I made were not solely based on personal taste, but also rooted in extensive research focused on easy navigation, effective content structure, and user-friendliness. By opting for a lighter color palette, I aimed to create a visually pleasing and inviting experience for visitors. The use of clean lines and ample white space enhances readability and highlights the key elements of my portfolio. The minimalist approach allows the focus to remain on the content itself, ensuring that my work takes center stage

Reflections

The process of building this portfolio has been a remarkable journey of personal growth and learning. Throughout the process, I discovered the importance of being open and flexible, willing to try different approaches and make adjustments along the way. As I delved into the world of coding, I encountered moments of frustration and uncertainty. However, each obstacle became an opportunity for growth and expansion of my coding skills. It was through trial and error, countless hours of research, and seeking guidance from online resources that I gradually transformed my vision into a tangible website.

Moving forward, I remain committed to refining my portfolio, continuously seeking ways to optimize its functionality and visual appeal. This ongoing process of improvement reflects my dedication to creating a captivating experience for readers and ensuring that my portfolio accurately represents my skills and accomplishments.
