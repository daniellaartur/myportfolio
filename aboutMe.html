About Me

Born and raised in Mozambique, a beautiful country located in the southern part of Africa. My name is Daniella and I am a UX/UI designer currently studying IT & Media Design at Fontys UAS, in The Netherlands.
From everyday conversations with different people across the globe, particularly with those I interacted the most in Africa, Europe and Asia, I became very passionate about human psychology. I am glad I found a branch in the technology field where I am able to design useful products by improving and/or creating great user experience to people, prioritising their needs and wishes.
