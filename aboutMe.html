Hi there! 👋

I was born and raised in Mozambique, a wonderful country located in southern east Africa.
My name is Daniella and I am an aspiring UX/UI designer currently finishing my bachelor's in ICT & Media Design at Fontys UAS, in The The Netherlands.
From everyday conversations with different people across the globe, particularly with those I interacted with the most in Africa, Europe and Asia, I became very passionate about human psychology. I am glad I found a branch in the technology field where I am able to design useful products by improving and/or creating great user experiences to people, prioritising their needs and wishes.
Beyond my academic pursuits as an almost graduate, I have a profound enthusiasm for exploring diverse cultures through travel and connecting with people around the world.

I'm always down to collaborate and improve my knowledge baggage. Reach out and let's chat! ☕
